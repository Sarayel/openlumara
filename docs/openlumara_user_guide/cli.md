# Using the Command Line (CLI) 💻

For the tech-savvy users or those who love a minimalist look, the CLI is a fantastic option.

We offer two ways to use the CLI:

1. **The Interactive CLI**: This is the full experience! It's colorful, shows you a beautiful history of your chats, and even gives you a clear, structured view of what the AI is doing when it uses its tools. It's like having a high-tech dashboard right in your terminal!
2. **The Simple CLI**: This is a lightweight, no-frills version. It's perfect for quick tasks or if you're running OpenLumara in a simple environment. It just takes your message and gives you the answer.

Whether you want a high-tech terminal or something simple and fast, we've got you covered! ⌨️
